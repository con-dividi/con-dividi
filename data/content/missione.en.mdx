---

The purpose of our association is to:

<ul class="list-disc">
	<li>
		<p>
			Ensure opportunities for people to gather over a snack and coffee, in
			order to get to know each other, engage in discussions, and support one
			another.
		</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<p>
			Giving voice and visibility to families by promoting their centrality
			through organizing initiatives, addressing specific issues, facilitating
			dialogues on specific topics of interest, and supporting family bonds.
		</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<p>
			Provide useful information or guidance and offer assistance when needed.
		</p>
	</li>
	<li>
		<p>
			Create suitable spaces to facilitate gatherings and expand a supportive
			network of connections.
		</p>
	</li>
</ul>
